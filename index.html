Recipe Finder by: David Vasquez

Hey there! Welcome to my recipe finder app, this app will help you find recipes based on what ingredients you have. So let's get started!

First, check the fridge and let's see what you have. Got your items ready? Great! Now, enter the ingredients that you have and enter them on in the text box to the right.

Ok, Once you've done that, click on the "Get recipe!" button and voila! You'll get your list recipes to try. The recipe titles will be clickable links that will be Google searches of that dish. I hope you find this helpful and that it brings out the inner-chef in you. Happy cooking!
